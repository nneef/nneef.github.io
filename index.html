Our Story

Nathan and Emily’s story began in Boston in March 2015. After building a strong friendship, they officially began dating in the fall of 2017, marking the start of a journey filled with love, laughter, and shared adventures. In 2023, Nathan proposed to Emily in Coralville, Iowa, surrounded by freshly fallen snow and eagles overhead—a perfect setting. Their relationship has thrived through their shared passions, from board game nights to serene hikes, from cozy picnics to evenings binge-watching their favorite TV shows. Now, surrounded by their closest friends and family, Nathan and Emily are thrilled to be tying the knot and beginning this exciting new chapter of their lives together.

Recommended

Hotels

The Hamilton, Atlanta

Located in the heart of downtown Atlanta, the Hamilton offers modern amenities and a comfortable stay just minutes away from popular attractions, dining, and entertainment. Guests will enjoy spacious rooms and complimentary Wi-Fi.

The Hilton, Atlanta

In downtown Atlanta, the Hilton combines luxury and convenience, providing a perfect home base for your visit. With stylish rooms, a fitness center, and a rooftop lounge with stunning city views, it’s a great spot to relax.

Things To Do

in Atlanta

The Georgia Aquarium

Dive into an unforgettable experience at the Georgia Aquarium, one of Atlanta’s must-visit attractions. Home to thousands of marine animals, including whale sharks, sea otters, and playful dolphins, it’s the perfect spot for guests of all ages.

The World of Coca-Cola

Discover the story behind one of the world’s most iconic brands at the World of Coca-Cola. Explore interactive exhibits, see vintage ads, and sample unique beverages from around the globe. Located downtown, it makes for a fun and fizzy afternoon!
